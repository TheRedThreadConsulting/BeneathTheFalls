The Discovery

It started as a rumor among the river guides—a light that glowed beneath the heavy curtain of the Niagara. When we finally broke through the silicate wall, we didn't find a cave; we found an ecosystem.

Vibrant amethysts pulsed with a rhythm like a heartbeat, casting a soft violet glow over emerald ferns that had never seen the sun. This was the "Enchanted Grotto," the soul of Beneath the Falls.
